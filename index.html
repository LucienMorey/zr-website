Zero Robotics Australia

A coding competition with a difference

Zero Robotics is a programming competition run by MIT Early rounds of the competition are played on NASA's simulator, but the final round of competition is played in space aboard the International Space Station. Since they are dealing with real systems students have to handle uncertainties in operation, code size limitations, and other real world engineering problems.

Thanks to a collaboration between MIT, NASA and The University of Sydney we are proud to provide Zero Robotics to all Australian schools free of charge!
